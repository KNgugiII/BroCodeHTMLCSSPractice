The aim of this lesson is to illustrate how videos can be added to webpages and their respective attributes.

001 - Without the CONTROLS video attribute, the video is visible but doesn't do much

002 - WITH THE CONTROLS video attribute, the video is visible & can be played

003 - WITH THE CONTROLS AUTOPLAY LOOP video attribute, the video is visible & just loops

004 - with a back up file, the browser will know which format to switch to incase the primary can't

005 - the video height or width can be set individually or in concert and the video plays

006 - Lastly, a video can be wrapped in an anchor element which redirects to another site
